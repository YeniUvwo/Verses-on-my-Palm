Salvation

Romans 10:9 — If you declare with your mouth, “Jesus is Lord,” and believe in your heart that God raised Him from the dead, you will be saved.

John 3:16 — For God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish but have eternal life.

Acts 4:12 — Salvation is found in no one else, for there is no other name under heaven given to mankind by which we must be saved.

Titus 3:5 — He saved us, not because of righteous things we had done, but because of His mercy. He saved us through the washing of rebirth and renewal by the Holy Spirit.

Prayer: Jesus, I believe You are the Son of God. I declare that You are Lord. I confess my sins and accept Your sacrifice. Wash me clean and be Lord over my life. Amen.

Now That I'm Saved

Galatians 2:20 — I have been crucified with Christ. It is no longer I who live, but Christ lives in me.

John 15:5 — “I am the vine; you are the branches. If you remain in Me and I in you, you will bear much fruit; apart from Me you can do nothing.”

1 Peter 2:2 — Like newborn babies, crave pure spiritual milk, so that by it you may grow up in your salvation.

Colossians 2:6-7 — So then, just as you received Christ Jesus as Lord, continue to live your lives in Him, rooted and built up in Him, strengthened in the faith as you were taught, and overflowing with thankfulness.

Next Steps:

  • Talk to God daily: Prayer keeps your relationship with Him alive.
  • Read the Bible regularly: Start with the Gospel of John or the book of Psalms.
  • Get baptized: A public declaration of your faith in Jesus.
  • Join a Bible-believing church: Fellowship and guidance help you grow spiritually.
  • Surround yourself with godly friends: Iron sharpens iron (Proverbs 27:17).
  • Live as a light: Share the Good News and walk in love and truth.

Prayer: Lord Jesus, I desire to grow in You. Lead me daily by Your Spirit. Strengthen my walk, connect me with the right community, and help me live boldly as Your child. Amen.

Love

Wealth:
Psalm 34:10 — The lions may grow weak and hungry, but those who seek the Lord lack no good thing.
I declare: I lack nothing. God supplies all I need. I live in overflow, blessing others from my abundance.

Faith:
Hebrews 11:1 — Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen.
I declare: My faith is alive and active. I trust God fully, even when I do not see. I walk by faith, not by sight.
