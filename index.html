Salvation

Romans 10:9 — That if you confess with your mouth the Lord Jesus and believe in your heart that God has raised Him from the dead, you will be saved.

John 3:16 — For God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life.

Prayer: Jesus, I believe You are the Son of God who died and rose again. I confess You as my Lord and Savior. I receive Your gift of eternal life. Wash me clean, fill me with Your Spirit, and help me to live for You.

New Me in Christ

2 Corinthians 5:17 — Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation; old things have passed away; behold, all things have become new.

Ephesians 4:24 — And that you put on the new man which was created according to God, in true righteousness and holiness.

Prayer: Lord Jesus, thank You for making me a new creation. Help me to walk in the newness of life, leaving behind the old ways. Clothe me daily with righteousness and holiness as I grow in You.

Now That I'm Saved

Colossians 2:6–7 — As you therefore have received Christ Jesus the Lord, so walk in Him, rooted and built up in Him and established in the faith, as you have been taught, abounding in it with thanksgiving.

Galatians 2:20 — I have been crucified with Christ; it is no longer I who live, but Christ lives in me; and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by faith in the Son of God, who loved me and gave Himself for me.

Next Steps:

  • Talk to God daily: Prayer keeps your relationship with Him alive.
  • Read the Bible regularly: Start with the Gospel of John or the book of Psalms.
  • Get baptized: A public declaration of your faith in Jesus.
  • Join a Bible-believing church: Fellowship and guidance help you grow spiritually.
  • Surround yourself with godly friends: Iron sharpens iron (Proverbs 27:17).
  • Live as a light: Share the Good News and walk in love and truth.

Prayer: Lord Jesus, I desire to grow in You. Lead me daily by Your Spirit. Strengthen my walk, connect me with the right community, and help me live boldly as Your child.

The Holy Spirit

Acts 1:8 — But you shall receive power when the Holy Spirit has come upon you; and you shall be witnesses to Me in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the end of the earth.

John 14:26 — But the Helper, the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in My name, He will teach you all things, and bring to your remembrance all things that I said to you.

Prayer: Holy Spirit, I welcome You into my life. Fill me with Your power to live as a witness for Christ. Teach me all things, and help me remember the words of Jesus. Guide, strengthen, and lead me daily.

Studying the Bible

Joshua 1:8 — This Book of the Law shall not depart from your mouth, but you shall meditate in it day and night, that you may observe to do according to all that is written in it. For then you will make your way prosperous, and then you will have good success.

James 1:22 — But be doers of the word, and not hearers only, deceiving yourselves.

Prayer: Father, give me a deep hunger for Your Word. Open my eyes by Your Spirit to understand it clearly. Help me to meditate on it, obey it, and let it shape how I live each day.

Soul Winning

Matthew 28:19–20 — Go therefore and make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things that I have commanded you; and lo, I am with you always, even to the end of the age. Amen.

Romans 10:14–15 — How then shall they call on Him in whom they have not believed? And how shall they believe in Him of whom they have not heard? And how shall they hear without a preacher? And how shall they preach unless they are sent? As it is written: "How beautiful are the feet of those who preach the gospel of peace, who bring glad tidings of good things!"

Prayer: Lord, fill me with boldness and compassion to share the Gospel. Let my life reflect Your love and truth. Use me as Your ambassador to reach those who are lost. Help me to be wise and faithful in winning souls for Your kingdom.

Praying

Matthew 6:6 — But you, when you pray, go into your room, and when you have shut your door, pray to your Father who is in the secret place; and your Father who sees in secret will reward you openly.

Philippians 4:6–7 — Be anxious for nothing, but in everything by prayer and supplication, with thanksgiving, let your requests be made known to God; and the peace of God, which surpasses all understanding, will guard your hearts and minds through Christ Jesus.

Prayer: Lord, draw me into daily communion with You. Teach me to pray with faith, thanksgiving, and persistence. Let Your peace rule in my heart, and help me to trust that You hear and answer.

Fasting

Matthew 6:16–18 — “Moreover, when you fast, do not be like the hypocrites, with a sad countenance. For they disfigure their faces that they may appear to men to be fasting. Assuredly, I say to you, they have their reward. But you, when you fast, anoint your head and wash your face, so that you do not appear to men to be fasting, but to your Father who is in the secret place; and your Father who sees in secret will reward you openly.”

Joel 2:12 — “Now, therefore,” says the Lord, “Turn to Me with all your heart, with fasting, with weeping, and with mourning.”

Prayer: Father, teach me to fast with the right heart,one that seeks You and not attention. Use my fasting to humble me, break every yoke, and draw me closer to You.

Forgiving

Matthew 6:14–15 — “For if you forgive men their trespasses, your heavenly Father will also forgive you. But if you do not forgive men their trespasses, neither will your Father forgive your trespasses.”

Prayer: Lord, I choose to forgive as You have forgiven me. Heal every wound in my heart. Give me the grace to let go of bitterness and walk in the freedom of love and peace

Prayer for the Person I Hurt: Lord, I admit I have caused pain. Please comfort and heal the heart I wounded. Give them strength to forgive, and give me humility to make things right. Let Your love restore what was broken.

Final Declaration

I am armed with the Word of God.
It is my sword, my shield, my strength.
When I am weak, it is my comfort.
When I am lost, it is my compass.
When I face battle, it is my victory.

I walk in faith, not by sight.
I stand in truth. I live by His Word.

Thank You Jesus!
